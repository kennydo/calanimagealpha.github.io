IRC Chatroom

The Cal Animage Alpha IRC channel is #CAA on irc.rizon.net. Many of our current and past members are extremely active in the chatroom.

Instructions

Want to set up your own IRC client so you can chat with club members without using the CAA website? Here's how:

  • Step 1: Download an IRC client. Popular choices are mIRC, HexChat, andChatzilla (a Firefox add-on).
  • Step 2: Connect to the CAA IRC channel by typing "/server irc.rizon.net" and then "/join #CAA".
  • Step 3: Have fun on the channel!
